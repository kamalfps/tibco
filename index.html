Our Philosophy

The TIBCO Standard

From our very first plunge of the AeroPress to the vibrant community we serve today in Surat, we exist to honor the artisanal journey from bean to cup. We refuse to compromise on quality.

Sourced

We do not follow commercial trends. We source unique beans, focusing on direct, transparent relationships with estates.

Crafted

Whether it is a precision pour-over or a dialed-in espresso, technique is the heart of our baristas' workflow.

Shared

Designed to be sanctuaries for creators, thinkers, and anyone seeking a moment of absolute peace in the city.
