Our Manifesto

01 / The Process

Independent Spirit

We do not follow commercial coffee trends. We independently source unique beans, focusing on transparent, direct relationships with farmers and estates to bring the purest flavors to Surat.

Brewer's Craft

Our baristas are artisans. Whether it is a precision pour-over or dialing in the perfect espresso shot, technique and respect for the bean is at the heart of everything we do.

Community First

Coffee is inherently meant to be shared. We have designed our spaces to be minimalist sanctuaries for creators, thinkers, and anyone seeking a moment of peace in the city.
