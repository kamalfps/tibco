Behind the Brew

Our Philosophy

We are The Independent Brewer's Coffee

The acronym TIBCO isn't just a name; it’s our manifesto. We champion the independence of the craft—sourcing ethically, roasting meticulously, and brewing without compromise. From our very first plunge of the AeroPress to the vibrant community we serve today in Surat, we exist to honor the artisanal journey from bean to cup.

Independent Spirit

We don't follow commercial coffee trends. We independently source unique beans, focusing on transparent relationships with farmers and estates.

Brewer's Craft

Our baristas are artisans. Whether it's a precision pour-over or dialing in the perfect espresso shot, technique is at the heart of everything we do.

Surat's Community

Coffee is meant to be shared. We’ve designed our spaces to be sanctuaries for creators, thinkers, and anyone seeking a moment of peace.
